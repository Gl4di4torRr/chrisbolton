About me

When I was twelve years old, I was interested in application development. My Dad was able to give me an HTML book and I started learning the basics. I created my first web application using a website template and editing a little HTML. In 2011, I began my journey by taking an introduction to Computer Science class. I quickly fell in love with the assignments and projects. After a couple of years in my Computer Science program, I decided to start looking for internships. I interviewed with The Home Depot and really enjoyed the culture, management and developers. After my internship in 2013, I gained loads of knowledge and enjoyed all of my co-workers, so I decided to join The Home Depot as a full-time associate. Now, I am a Senior Software Engineer at The Home Depot. I enjoy full stack development, in particular, I love the Java programming language. I also enjoy the extreme programming practices and automation to help deliver products and features to production more quickly!
